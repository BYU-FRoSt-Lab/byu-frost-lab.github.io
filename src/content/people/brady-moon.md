Brady Moon completed his Ph.D. in [Robotics](https://ri.cmu.edu) at [Carnegie Mellon University](https://cmu.edu) and joined BYU as an Assistant Professor in July 2025. His areas of expertise include autonomous information gathering, motion planning, and machine learning for field robotics. His current research interests include task and path planning, autonomous systems, multi-agent coordination, human-robot interaction, and the development of adaptive, reliable, and genuinely useful robotic systems for real-world applications such as agriculture, disaster response, and infrastructure inspection.
