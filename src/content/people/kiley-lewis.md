# About

Kiley Lewis is a Master’s student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department. She graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering in April 2023 from BYU.

Kiley started doing research with the FROST Lab in April 2021, which is where she got her first exposure to robotics. She mainly focused on hardware and software for the autonomous wheelchair project. She also helped with hardware for various vehicles in the lab. Kiley is excited to further her career in robotics by continuing to work on various underwater vehicles in order to complete a Master’s Thesis.

Outside of school, Kiley enjoys spending time outside and being active. She enjoys playing basketball, frisbee, volleyball, and any other sport. She also loves spending time with her husband and son.
