# About
Braden Meyers is an undergraduate in BYU's mechanical engineering program with a minor in economics. He has competed in the departments robotics competition for several years. He enjoys 3D printing and PCB design and uses that in his personal projects. Aside from engineering he spends time outside and training for marathons.

# Research
Braden's research is focused on designing and modeling a low-cost underwater autonomous vehicle that can be easily built to enable underwater terrain navigation to be performed with a fleet of these vehicles communicating with each other.
